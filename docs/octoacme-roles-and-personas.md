# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## Design Lead / UX Designer

### Role Summary
Design Leads and UX Designers own the user experience design process to ensure products are intuitive, accessible, and meet user needs. They translate user research and business requirements into visual designs and interaction patterns.

### Responsibilities
- Develop wireframes, mockups, and prototypes for new features
- Conduct UX research and usability testing
- Create and maintain design guidelines, systems, and asset libraries
- Collaborate with Product Managers, Developers, and Stakeholders on requirements and feedback
- Ensure accessibility and inclusive design principles are applied
- Validate that implemented features match design specifications

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive and accessible user experiences
- Establish consistent design patterns across products
- Reduce user friction and support costs through thoughtful UX

### Typical Communication
- Share early designs and gather feedback in planning meetings
- Participate in sprint demos/reviews to validate implementation
- Provide design critiques during PR reviews
- Present user research findings to inform product decisions

---

## Quality Assurance (QA) Lead

### Role Summary
QA Leads coordinate testing activities and quality verification to ensure products meet acceptance criteria and maintain high reliability standards. They work across the team to define test strategies and validate releases.

### Responsibilities
- Define and maintain comprehensive QA test plans and test cases
- Execute manual and automated testing across features
- Manage bug tracking, triage, and resolution workflows
- Verify that acceptance criteria are met before releases
- Work with Developers and Product Managers to ensure appropriate test coverage
- Identify quality risks and recommend mitigations

### Goals
- Prevent defects from reaching production
- Maintain high test coverage and reliability
- Reduce mean time to detect and resolve issues

### Typical Communication
- Report testing progress and blockers in daily standups
- Summarize quality status and sign-off readiness ahead of releases
- Document test results and provide detailed bug reports
- Participate in retrospectives with quality metrics and improvement suggestions

---

## DevOps / Infrastructure Engineer

### Role Summary
DevOps and Infrastructure Engineers ensure smooth operation of build, deployment, and infrastructure processes. They enable reliable, automated delivery and maintain system health in production.

### Responsibilities
- Design, implement, and maintain CI/CD pipelines
- Monitor system health, performance metrics, and alerts
- Implement infrastructure as code and configuration management
- Manage deployment processes and release automation
- Partner in incident response, recovery, and post-mortems
- Optimize build times and deployment reliability

### Goals
- Enable fast, safe, automated deployments
- Minimize production incidents and downtime
- Improve observability and system reliability

### Typical Communication
- Sync with Developers and QA on release planning and deployment windows
- Respond to and coordinate production incidents
- Share infrastructure updates and capacity planning in team meetings
- Document runbooks and deployment procedures

---

## Business Analyst

### Role Summary
Business Analysts map business needs into actionable technical and process steps. They bridge communication between stakeholders and technical teams, ensuring requirements are clear and aligned with business objectives.

### Responsibilities
- Gather, document, and validate business requirements
- Analyze data, user feedback, and market trends
- Create process flows, user stories, and requirement specifications
- Facilitate communication between business stakeholders and technical teams
- Validate that delivered solutions meet business objectives
- Track success metrics and report on business outcomes

### Goals
- Ensure clear, actionable requirements
- Align technical delivery with business value
- Improve decision-making through data analysis

### Typical Communication
- Participate in planning meetings to clarify requirements
- Document process flows, business rules, and requirements updates
- Present data analysis and insights to stakeholders
- Bridge technical and non-technical discussions

---

## Support Lead / Customer Success

### Role Summary
Support Leads and Customer Success managers champion the voice of the customer pre- and post-launch. They ensure customers are informed about changes and that customer feedback informs product improvements.

### Responsibilities
- Communicate releases, changes, and updates to end users
- Triage incoming feedback, support requests, and feature requests
- Coordinate with Product and Project Managers to prioritize customer-impacting issues
- Create and maintain customer-facing documentation and FAQs
- Track customer satisfaction metrics and support trends
- Represent customer needs in planning and prioritization discussions

### Goals
- Maximize customer satisfaction and retention
- Reduce support burden through proactive communication
- Ensure customer voice influences product decisions

### Typical Communication
- Share support trends and customer pain points in retrospectives
- Collaborate during release preparation to plan customer communications
- Participate in incident resolution to coordinate customer updates
- Provide customer feedback summaries in planning sessions
